Episode X: A New Hope

Date Sun 03 August 2014

It's 6 o'clock in the morning as I stumble out of the office on Thursday/Friday night. I had waged war with Darth Vader for the last three nights, but this time, I had done it. Vader guards the network with the packet filter, an elegant firewall for a more civilized age. And as I go to bed, I'm excited to tell Matt that we can finally leave the gateway in place for testing.

Of course, when I plugged it in the next day to show him, no ones' computers stayed connected.

That's been the story of last week. But, the gateway does work now, in almost every way I wanted to. Matt was only here a short time last week, but I was really excited that he noticed it's faster. And there's software to monitor who's using lots of the internet. And not a moment too soon, because I'm leaving this Friday.

Now all I've got left is documentation, which isn't particularly exciting, but is possibly the most important part of the summer. When I leave, Matt is going to have to be able to figure out how it all works. I need prayers that I can finish strong in this final stretch before I go home.
